About Kylee Larsen

Kylee Larsen, born Kylee Keetch, was born and raised for the first 8 years of her life in Salt Lake City, Utah. At 8-years-old her parents moved her and her three siblings out to Grantsville, Utah. Here Kylee played every sport possible, finally settling on playing tennis, basketball, and golf in High School. Kylee completed High School at the top of her class of 2011. Upon completion of High School, Kylee accepted a full-ride scholarship down to Snow College in Ephraim, Utah and completed her Associates Degree of Science in her first year with help from all the concurrent enrollment courses completed during High School. Kylee spent the next 8 years raising her only daughter in Salt Lake City, Utah and working customer service jobs and sales jobs. Kylee became a single mother in 2018, despite surviving a physically and emotionally abusive marriage, Kylee was determined to provide the happiest, healthiest life for herself and her daughter. The year 2020 and the global pandemic changed things for EVERYONE. Kylee knew this was a turning point, and after losing a customer service position she knew was below her, Kylee applied to Utah State University and was accepted as an online student. She started courses and completed two semesters before realizing this wasn't the major for her. Kylee applied and was accepted into The Jon M. Hunstman School of Business and chose to Major in Marketing. Kylee began taking the Business courses online and in her first Intro to Data course, something clicked. She loved coding and programming. It was FUN! A quick meeting with an academic advisor and a few easy swaps, Kylee knew Information Systems was the Major for her. Kylee began to thrive in her courses and her confidence grew. Web Development was where she has always been meant to end up.
